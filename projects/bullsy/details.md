## Overview

Bullsy is a minimalist focus and productivity tracker designed to help you stay on top of your tasks without the bloat of traditional heavy-weight time managers.

### Features
- Clean, dark-themed UI.
- Pomodoro timer integration.
- Daily goal tracking.
- Session logs.

### Tech Stack
- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Build Tool: Vite
- Deployment: Vercel / Netlify

### Thought Process
I created Bullsy because I wanted a focus app that didn't distract me with complex charts or paywalls. By focusing on essentialism, the app allows users to get straight into flow state.
